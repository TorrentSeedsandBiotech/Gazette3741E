Gazette Notification No.: S.O. 3741(E)

Notification Date: 13/08/2025

Title of Biostimulant:

Humic acid and Fulvic acid 85% (Powder)

Composition:

Sr. No. Ingredients Content (%)
1 Humic acid (Source: Leonardite) per cent. by weight, minimum 70%
2 Fulvic acid per cent. by weight, minimum 15%
3 Potassium hydroxide per cent. by weight 15%
4 Total (per cent.) 100%

Recommended Crop: Tomato

Recommended Dose: Three soil applications at 1250 g/ha.
